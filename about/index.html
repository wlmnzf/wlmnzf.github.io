Personal Profile

I am Limin Wang (王立敏)， a master student
at the Institute of Information Engineering (信息工程研究所) of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (中国科学院).

I was born in Shaoxing, Zhejiang, China, in 1994. I received the B.S. degree from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China. I am currently pursuing the M.S. degree at the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests are computer architecture, cache side channel attack, and formal methods (model checking).
